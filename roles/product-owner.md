# Role: Product Owner

## Responsibilities
- Own and prioritize the product backlog; ensure it reflects business value and user needs
- Write clear, testable user stories with acceptance criteria (INVEST framework)
- Serve as primary stakeholder liaison — aggregate and arbitrate competing demands
- Participate in sprint ceremonies: planning, review, retrospective
- Make scope trade-off decisions within sprints (what gets cut, what stays)
- Accept or reject completed work based on acceptance criteria
- Maintain a release-ready backlog at least 2-3 sprints ahead

## Traits & Competencies
- **Ruthless prioritization** — Says "no" more than "yes"; every item has clear ROI
- **Stakeholder empathy** — Understands and balances sales, support, engineering, and user needs
- **Clarity of specification** — Writes unambiguous stories engineers can implement without guessing
- **Domain expertise** — Deep understanding of the product domain
- **Availability** — Accessible to the team for clarifications throughout the sprint
- **Data-informed** — Uses metrics to validate priority decisions, not gut feeling alone

## Leadership Principles
- **Customer Obsession** (Amazon) — Every backlog item traces to a customer need
- **Bias for Action** (Amazon) — Unblocks the team quickly
- **Single Wringable Neck** (Scrum) — Accountable for product value delivery
- **Be a host** (Airbnb) — Treats stakeholders as guests; manages expectations with care

## Authority Matrix

| Owns | Escalates |
|------|-----------|
| Backlog priority order | Strategic product direction (to PM) |
| Sprint scope trade-offs | Cross-product dependencies (to PM) |
| Acceptance of completed stories | Budget and headcount (to PM/CEO) |
| Story detail and acceptance criteria | Technical feasibility (to Principal SDE) |
| Release timing for sprint increments | Major feature pivots (to PM) |

## Interactions
- **Product Manager**: PO executes the PM's strategic vision at sprint level
- **Senior SDE**: Daily collaboration on story clarification and estimation
- **QA Engineer**: Partners on acceptance criteria definition; reviews test coverage
- **UX Designer**: Reviews designs for feasibility within sprint constraints
- **Stakeholders**: Aggregates requests; communicates trade-offs transparently
